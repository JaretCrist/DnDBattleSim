Battle Simulator

Welcome to the DnD 5th Edition Battle Simulator!

Controls:
  • W or Up : Move Cursor Up
  • A or Left : Move Cursor Left
  • S or Down : Move Cursor Down
  • D or Right : Move Cursor Right
  • E, V, O, or Enter : Confirm / Select
  • R, C, P, Backspace : Cancel
  • Mouse controls coming in later update!
