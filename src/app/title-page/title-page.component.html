Battle Simulator

Welcome to the DnD 5th Edition Battle Simulator!

Controls:
  • Cursor Controls: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Confirm / Select: Enter
  • Cancel: Backspace
  • Menu Navigation: Tab or Click
  • Full mouse controls: Now Available!
Color Meanings:
  • Black outline around token: current character
  • Purple square: space within movement reach
  • Orange box outline: default cursor
  • Green box outline: movement cursor
  • Yellow box outline: attack cursor
